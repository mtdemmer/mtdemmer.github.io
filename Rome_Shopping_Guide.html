Porta PorteseFlea MarketsTrastevereOpen SunSunday onlyStart at Piazza Ippolito Nievo, 00153 RomaThe author's Sunday-morning ritual for 3+ years. Begin at Piazza Ippolito Nievo, not Porta Portese itself. Hug the left path, loop back, then take the next road. Something for everyone — vintage glass ashtrays, travel ephemera, clothing; not great for furniture. Go before 10 in winter, before 8:30 in summer (heat/crowds by 10:30). Dress down, bring a canvas bag, learn 'quanto costa?' Best score: a green Gucci letter holder for 15 euros.
